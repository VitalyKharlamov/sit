How it works

  • Schools and PE teachers web app
  • Schools public web site and big screen
  • Schools PE staff mobile app
  • Parents and students mobile app
  • Tournaments and events web site
  • Schools sports partnerships web site
Schools and PE teachers web app

• See your children personalised fixtures calendar
• See games results and match reports in real time
• See your children performance in their favourite sports
• Get notified about last minutes changes
• Send availability reports and replay consent requests anytime, anywhere
• Communicate with school PE staff and other parents

Schools public web site and big screen

• Modern design, photo gallery on the landing page
• Highlighted events in the focus
• School sport news
• All pupils details are hidden for data privacy
• School fixtures calendar, games results, reports and photos are published automatically

Schools PE staff mobile app

• See your school fixtures calendar
• Input games results and match reports from pitches
• See pupils emergency contacts even when out of the internet connection
• Get notified about pupils unavailability to take part
• Send notifications parents and students about last minutes changes

Parents and students mobile app

• See personalised fixtures calendar
• See games results and match reports in real time
• Track the performance in favourite sports
• Get notified about last minutes changes
• Send availability reports and replay consent requests anytime, anywhere
• Communicate with school PE staff and other parents and students

Tournaments and events web site

• Well-designed groups results and finals pages
• Games results presented automatically once submitted
• Dedicated web-site for each event hosted on Squad In Touch servers for free
• Detailed results for each school which participated
• Printable and email-adjusted PDFs with overall tournament results

Schools sports partnerships web site

• Modern design, photo gallery on the landing page
• Seasonal tables are published automatically once games results are submitted
• Highlighted events in the focus
• SSP sport news
• Dedicated web-site for each event hosted on Squad In Touch servers for free
