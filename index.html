What is Sports Stars Inspiration Day

The Olympic Inspiration day is a time for pupils to raise their bar of aspiration and ambition. It is a time for pupils eyes to be opened to new goals and experience new challenges led by our team of Olympians.

Team of Olympians

Brendan Reilly
Double Olympian, qualified PE teacher and Dad of 3 sports mad kids
Brendan wears many hats when it comes to sports development. Brendan has delivered 100's of these School Sports Days over a 25 year period and is now helping other Olympians develop their work in schools to inspire pupils nationwide.
