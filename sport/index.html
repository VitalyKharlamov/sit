What is Sports Stars Inspiration Day?

The Sport Stars Inspiration Day is a time for pupils to raise their bar of aspiration and ambition. It is a time for pupils eyes to be opened to new goals and experience new challenges led by our team of Olympians.

Double Olympian, Qualified PE Teacher and Dad of 3 sports mad kids
Brendan wears many hats when it comes to sports development. He has delivered 100's of these School Sports Days over a 25 year period and is now helping other Olympians develop their work in schools to inspire pupils nationwide.

Team

Martyn Bernard
One of the most successful British High Jumper ever
Martyn is an Olympic finalist as well as a Commonwealth games medallist with a personal best of 2.3m. Martyn is the perfect example of having a ‘plan B’ away from the athletics track. He graduated from psychology and has recently started a law degree. He has interests in film and an honorary director of the Film production Company, "21st Century Filmworks Ltd", A Fucilla Production Company, and co-producer of the film “Fields of Gold” currently in pre production stage.

An inspiration on many levels.

Team

Mel Clarke
Double Para-Olympian medallist
She has also won Gold and Silver medals at the World Championships. Due to an arthritic condition Mel has had to use a wheelchair since the age of 11 and lost sight in her aiming eye and had to remodel her approach physically and mentally.

Mel has an amazing story that pupils can relate to due to the age she was whilst adapting to school, society and sport. A true hero of resilience and of British Sport.
